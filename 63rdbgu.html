Grand Procession

Experience the Magnificent Grand Procession

Witness the most spectacular event of the 63rd Bengaluru Ganesha Utsava - a grand procession that will traverse through the heart of Basavanagudi starting from BMS College of Engineering to Ramakrishna Aashram Circle through Gandhi Bazar Circle and DVG Road. Ending at APS College grounds, showcasing the divine presence of Lord Ganesha in all His glory. This magnificent procession marks the culmination of our 11-day celebration with unprecedented grandeur and devotion.

Divine Decorations

Elaborately decorated palanquin with traditional ornaments, flowers, and sacred symbols

Exiquisite Performances

A vibrant procession featuring traditional percussion, skilled drummers, talented artists, captivating folklore stunts, and 14 exceptional teams from across India comes together to create a spectacular celebration.

Mass Participation

Thousands of devotees joining the procession with joy, flowers, and prayers

Join the Divine Celebration

Be part of this historic grand procession and receive the blessings of Lord Ganesha. Experience the divine energy, cultural richness, and spiritual awakening that makes BGU a truly unique celebration.
